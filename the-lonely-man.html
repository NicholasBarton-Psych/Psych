Is there a male loneliness epidemic?

The Lonely Man

There is undeniably a loneliness epidemic occurring amongst the young modern population. These are the factors causing this as well as the interventions you can take to combat it and why it can be most impactful for men.

1. Technology

Many reasons
Technology has removed the need for in person interaction
Incapacity to self regulate and be bored, be alone
People on phones not talking to each other - be present in idle conversation

2. Decline of male social groups

Social gatherings, meetings, clubs have reduced by this percentage

3. Fewer long term deep relationships

Movements such as the incel movement has demonised women, there is an increasing gap between men and women, relationships are becoming less deep, more infrequent, less impactful

4. The lonely chapter

Now if you’ve chosen this path, this one you can’t do much about. Many young professionals chose this road. The only practical advice I’ve heard to combat this, was from the evolutionary psychologist … who said to get a pet (real advice).
